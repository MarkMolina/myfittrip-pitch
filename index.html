Sports, Wellness & Wellbeing Anywhere

Currently, there is no comprehensive platform that collects and enables the booking of sports, wellness, and wellbeing travel packages worldwide.

These offerings are primarily discovered through social media networks or word of mouth.

But more specifically, this travel market is offline, fragmented and unstructructured.

While it's the fastest growing segment within tourism.

Our Solution

MyFitTrip.com is the Booking.com for Sports, Wellness & Wellbeing travel.

Our platform allows travelers to easily find, compare, and book these packages while ensuring high-quality standards.

Our Solution

With our unique marketing setup, we empower Sports, Wellness & Wellbeing influencers to easily create, promote and sell their preferred retreats through our platform. They receive a personalized social network link and enjoy 10-50% commission or kickback on bookings. This minimizes marketing costs and expands reach to an engaged audience.

Our Opportunity

The global wellness tourism market size is expected to reach
USD 1,542.6 billion by 2030, with a CAGR of 7.2% from 2023 to 2030.

Rising awareness about health and personal care is driving the market, with promotional campaigns by governments, encouraging wellness tourism.

This aligns with the UN SDG goal 3 - Ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages

Increasing demand for wellness activities at popular travel destinations, including spas, mineral springs, skiing, hiking, yoga, and more.

Boris Mulder / Mark Molina

There is no comprehensive platform that collects and enables the booking of sports, wellness, and wellbeing travel packages worldwide.

These offerings are primarily discovered through social media networks or word of mouth.

Specifically, this travel market is fragmented and unstructructured.

But it's a fast growing segment within the tourism industry.

Our Solution

MyFitTrip.com is building the booking.com for sports, wellness, and wellbeing travel.

Our Solution

MyFitTrip.com is building the booking.com for sports, wellness, and wellbeing travel.

Our platform allows travelers to easily find, compare, and book these packages while ensuring high-quality standards.

Additionally, we enable sports, wellness, and wellbeing enthusiasts, influencers, and trainers to create and promote their own retreats through our platform, offering a link to their social network and providing them with a commission discount.

Our Opportunity

The global wellness tourism market size is expected to reach

Rising awareness about health and personal care is driving the market, with promotional campaigns by governments encouraging wellness tourism.

Increasing demand for wellness activities at popular travel destinations, including spas, mineral springs, skiing, hiking, yoga, and more.

Business Model

Revenue generation: 15% commission on total booking value (average booking value: 1300 EUR).

Partnerships with sports, wellness, and wellbeing brands for advertisement fees, affiliate marketing, or revenue sharing.

Target audience acquisition through SEO, paid search, and leveraging social media and online communities of sports, wellness, and wellbeing enthusiasts.

Providing a share of the commission to influencers for each booking, minimizing marketing costs.

Financial Projections

Short-term: Revenue generated through a 15% commission on bookings. Average booking value is €1,500.

Long-term: Revenue streams include strategic partnerships, advertising, sponsored content, and premium subscriptions. Revenue estimations provided for the first five years.
